Executive Summary

Telefonica UK has been delivering revenue and customer growth since 2017

Telefonica UK is projected to eclipse £6b in revenue in 2019 and is one of 4 key markets to focus in the company’s 5-point plan. These consistent results are driven by its O2 brand recognition, strong customer service, and innovative commercial strategies such as Custom Plans in Contract and Big Bundles, a simple PAYG offer with no tariffs.

Moreover, with the advent of 5G technology, O2 is positioned to expand its product portfolio to create and diversify new revenue stream from customers and businesses.

However, generating enough funding is critical for 5G investments and realise future growth

The market is more competitive than ever; customers can easily switch companies and major carriers are already offering unlimited data plans. Companies need to find other ways to stand out and differentiate themselves.

Although in decline and low in ARPU, PAYG can still unlock more value, because is a source of contract customers and it offers the flexibility that today’s customer wants. However, the top-up experience is time-consuming, hampering the opportunity to capture more revenue and build direct relationship with each customer, two important factors that ensure funding for ongoing investments.

How can Telefonica UK drive revenue to fund future investments and solidify customer loyalty?

Telefonica UK can simplify its Pay As You Go product one step further: scrap top ups altogether and integrate payment capabilities in existing AI conversational chatbot to capture revenue instantly and provide an easier and quicker service that suits today’s customer expectations. We call it Pay As You Go+
