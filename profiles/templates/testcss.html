Executive Summary

  1. Since 2016, Telefonica UK has been growing revenue and number of customers, thanks to O2 brand recognition, customer loyalty and innovative commercial strategies.
  2. However, generating enough funding is critical for ongoing 5G investments and realise Telefonica UK’s digital transformation.
  3. What can Telefonica UK do to drive revenue to fund future investments, solidify customer loyalty and enlarge gap to competition?
  4. Telefonica UK can simplify its Pay As You Go service by scrapping top ups altogether and integrating payment capabilities to existing AI chatbot to capture revenue instantly and provide an easier and quicker service that suits today’s customer expectations.
